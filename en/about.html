President of Arab University

President's Speech

We were keen to have a private university in Hadhramaut, originating in its territory, meeting all the aspirations of its children to obtain quality education efficiently and effectively, based on distinguished scientific foundations and environment. We strive to contribute to qualifying secondary school graduates and upgrading diploma holders to obtain a bachelor's degree, and qualifying the youth of the community to contribute to meeting the needs of the labor market and the requirements of development and renaissance.

We are keen for Arab University to be among the prestigious universities that reflect the identity of its land and contribute to building the human being, through which the civilization and culture of Hadhramaut, science, and history are manifested.

Dr. Aboud Mohammed Al-Abal

University President

About University

Established 2017 - 2018

University Definition

Arab University was established in the academic year 2017-2018 under Ministerial Decree No. (4) of 2018 by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. The establishment of the university came as a contribution to accommodating the outputs of general secondary school students and its equivalent. The university seeks to achieve the desired leadership in higher education by keeping pace with advanced educational curricula and presenting them within scientific and research programs.

  • Academic programs that adopt quality standards and institutional accreditation.
  • Modern educational environment supporting scientific research and community service.
  • Active partnerships with professional sectors locally and internationally.

Vision

A distinguished and leading university in higher education and community service.

Mission

Providing renewable scientific and research programs that keep pace with advanced higher education curricula and serve the community.

Strategic Objectives

01

Empowering Competencies

Graduating scientific competencies capable of competing and integrating into the regional labor market.

02

Stimulating Environment

Creating a modern educational environment that supports creativity and community participation and enhances belonging.

03

Research & Innovation

Expanding the scientific research and innovation system to serve the community and raise the quality of knowledge outputs.

04

Quality & Accreditation

Aligning academic and administrative systems with national and international accreditation requirements.

05

Effective Governance

Rational management based on transparency, data, and accountability to achieve sustainability.

06

Strategic Partnerships

Building a network of local and international relationships that enhance training and employment opportunities and knowledge transfer.
